DIWALI:THE FESTIVAL OF LIGHTS

Diwali, the five-day "Festival of Lights," celebrates the victory of light over darkness and good over evil, with its date varying annually based on the Hindu lunar calendar. Celebrated by Hindus, Sikhs, Jains, and Buddhists, it involves lighting lamps and candles, decorating homes with rangoli (colored designs), wearing new clothes, feasting, exchanging gifts, and performing prayers for the goddess Lakshmi and god Ganesha.
