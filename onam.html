ONAM:HARVEST FESTIVAL OF KERALA

Onam is the harvest festival of Kerala, celebrated to commemorate the return of the mythical King Mahabali. This ten-day festival, which occurs in the Malayalam month of Chingam (August-September), features flower rangolis (Pookalam), a grand vegetarian feast (Onasadya), and lively festivities including boat races (Vallam Kali) and traditional dances. The celebrations symbolize prosperity, unity, and Kerala's rich cultural heritage, and are observed by Malayalis worldwide, regardless of caste or religion.
